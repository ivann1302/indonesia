By The Numbers

Indonesia is an amazing country with rich culture, unique nature and incredible diversity. Discover the impressive numbers that make Indonesia one of the most interesting places on the planet.

100+

Active volcanoes in Indonesia, which accounts for 13% of all volcanoes in the world. This makes the country one of the most geologically active regions on the planet.

43,000+

Square kilometers are occupied by Bali's rice terraces, creating stunning landscapes that are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

30+

Unique animal species live only in Indonesia, including orangutans, Komodo dragons and many endemic bird species.
